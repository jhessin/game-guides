## Fish food
x10
Recipe for fish food supporting linguistic development (prepare fresh daily)
- 1 ounce of flour
- 1 worm
- 1 white mushroom
- 1 egg
- 1 sling herb

Blend carefully and sprinkle into water. Repeat words which you want the fish to learn.
